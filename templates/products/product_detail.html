Care Instructions

1. Unpack Gently
Cautiously remove the bouquet using scissors and any outer plastic or silk from around the stems. Be cautious while doing so to let the flowers breathe and bloom naturally.
2. Trim the Stems
Cut 2-3 inches of the stems at a 45-degree angle to help them absorb water more effectively.
3. Prepare the Vase
Fill a clean vase with fresh water. For extra nourishment, add a teaspoon of sugar and a few drops of lemon juice or use the included flower food.
4. Remove Dying Foliage
Pluck off any leaves that will sit below the waterline to prevent bacterial growth.
5. Choose the Right Spot
Place your flowers in a cool area with indirect sunlight â€“ away from heat, direct sun, or cool drafts.
6. Refresh Regularly
Change the water every two days and re-trim the stems to keep your blooms fresher for longer.
